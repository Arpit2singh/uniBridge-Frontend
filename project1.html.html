About Us

UniBridge is a platform designed to enhance communication between students and the university administration. Our system provides real-time feedback, poll-based voting mechanisms, issue reporting, and access to study resources. UniBridge strives to create a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly experience for both students and university staff.
